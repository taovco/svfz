Unread post by SearchVoat » Tue Feb 02, 2021 1:18 am

My firm commitment is to allow all US-legal speech here.

Motivated by my desire to preserve the site as a free speech bastion, I implemented NSFW (somewhat like Voat). If some random follows a link here and sees "I hate niggers" they may decide we deserve to be attacked - via DDOS or subversion or pile-on or however - and I would prefer not to have to apply time, resources and motivation to dealing with that.

Obviously it would be great to ignore that risk and just speak freely to the world. But there are practical considerations. Seemed like a no-brainer.

Then I was surprised to see opposition to the categorization of so-called "offensive" material here as NSFW. (I use "offensive" in quotes because obviously we're not all "offended" by the same things.)

This isn't a democracy, I can't promise to be bound by the votes here, but I do want to hear the arguments and encourage the debate.
