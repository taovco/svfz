I saw someone here mention it (forgot who) so I checked it out, and it's easily the closest thing to voat re culture, content, and format. I spend more time lurking there each day. If you don't know about it, give it a try. It's easier to use than poal, is definitely less faggysoybeta than poal, so far I haven't seen any signs of censorship or over-moderation (time will tell in this department), the content is just as based as voat, and it appears to have a pretty good sized userbase. And for some combination of these same reasons, it is so far superior to goatpen and notabug as well.

So, there it is. Try it out if you feel so inclined. I will of course still chill here with you goats, because I like it here too.

https://consumeproduct.win

EDIT: As I explore the site more today, I am learning that consumeproduct.win is just a small section of a much larger community, including the once thedonald.win, now patriots.win, a big Qtard section, Kotakuinaction stuff, and more. Some of the memes are stale and boomerish, but overall, the site seems to be cool enough. I have not performed a free speech test there yet, still lurking to see if I can figure out which sections of the site are legit and which might be pozzed.
