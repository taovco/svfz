WASHINGTON (ChurchMilitant.com) - A medical institute directed by Dr. Anthony Fauci is paying more than $200,000 to use monkeys in so-called transgender studies.

The National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID), a division of the National Institutes of Health (NIH), paid $205,562 to Scripps Research in December to ascertain why gender-confused men have high rates of HIV. NIH confirms the study slated for this year involves injecting male monkeys with feminizing hormones to determine how the hormone therapy impacts the monkeys' immune systems.

One neuroscientist, Dr. Katherine Roe, labeled the study "ineffective" because monkeys can't contract HIV.


Dr. Katherine Roe

It's unclear why NIAID is paying to determine why biological men, who are so-called transgender women, have a higher incidence of HIV as men who identify as women typically have sex with other men; the CDC already reports gay and bisexual men have a far greater chance of contracting HIV. Studies show transgender people are nearly 50 times more at risk of getting HIV compared to the general population.

But NIAID also awarded $272,626 in taxpayer funding to Scripps Research in 2021 to likewise give monkeys transgender hormones and "see what happens."

A year after Catholic-raised Fauci took charge of NIAID in 1984, the agency began HIV vaccine experiments on monkeys. Reports further show a pharmaceutical company provides the health institute with approximately 500 rhesus monkeys each year, to the tune of $27.5 million.

Roe also noted it was "yet another pointless, wasteful monkey torture experiment."
