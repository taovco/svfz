There are no rules about modding.

In practice, anyone can ask for any new sub and will probably get it. Sub owners are free to ban users and delete posts at will, without providing any reason.

This can obviously lead to deletions and bans based on animosity towards a user, not their content. This is made worse by the absence of subverse mod logs.

I'm considering implementing proper mod logs and making some formal rules about modding. In the meantime I'm considering putting flags on subverses which have been known to mod based on animosity, not content.

Please let me know if you believe you have been unfairly banned or blocked from a subverse based on personal animosity, not content.
