I’m not an SDA, and I can’t remember the details of the whole thesis now, but from what I recall it included the idea that the name Michael meaning “Who is like God”, or even “He that is what God is,” could only belong to the son of God.
There are also many interpretations of various scriptures which construe the apparent great power, and high standing, and the juxtaposition of Michael with Satan, as being presented in terms that could only apply to Yahusha.
I looked into the idea quite a while ago now, and came away feeling unconvinced but not sure in myself what I believe.
As much as there were verses which seemed to make a very plausible case, there were others which clearly seemed to refute it.
I guess it’s time to have another look.
