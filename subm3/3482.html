Warwick Police Identify Body Found Floating In Greenwich Bay




WARWICK, RI — First responders Wednesday at around 7 a.m. responded to the beach at the end
of Nausauket Road for a body found floating 50 yards offshore in Greenwich Bay.

The Warwick Fire Depart retrieved the male victim and brought him to shore. James D. Kimball,
49, of the Nausauket area of Warwick, was pronounced dead at Kent County Memorial Hospital,
Warwick Police Capt. Ryan Sornberger said in a news release.

Witnesses in the area reported that the man was previously seen digging for quahogs.

The cause of death will be determined by the medical examiner. However,the incident does not
appear to be suspicious, Sornberger said.
