You might get that on a VPN or over TOR or if you've exceeded some kind of search limit, if you DM me the IP address I can check.

The forum and search servers are entirely separate and don't share data in real time, so search cannot use your forum sign-in.

If you need to sign in, use the special SearchVoat password sent to your Voat Inbox - if you've changed your password on the forum it won't be recognised for search.

Hope that helps, let me know, can continue this by Private Message if you prefer.
