The way to include a picture is simply to upload the pic to an image hosting site like catbox.moe and then take the url it gives you and paste it into your submission. Also, you can do a SearchVoat of submissions on Gosch, pick one that contains this evidence, and paste that url (from your browser window at the top of your browser page) into the text.

If you want to get fancy and paste it as an embedded link, i.e. to turn the words Johnny Gosch into the hyperlink to the previous Voat research, you highlight the words you want to turn into an embedded link and click the button that looks like a chain, above the text window. That inserts the php formatting code. Then you want to put your cursor just inside the first closed bracket, type an = sign and immediately paste in the url you want to embed, no spaces. You can click "Preview" to make sure it turned out right.
