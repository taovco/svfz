I rarely posted to any sub besides QRV. People tend to judge, hold grudges etc. Not interested in followers or haters or whatever. Probably will not participate much without anonymity.

Edit: I would like to try to clarify for those who “don’t get it.” Firstly, these boards keep every post and comment in the user profile so that any user can look and see the history of any other user. Just yesterday on GA.win I saw a comment along the lines of, “well, don’t put too much stock in username because she sometimes goes off half-cocked.” I would prefer that my words be judged on their merit alone, not on my post/comment history. As should everyones. We should be allowed to make mistakes, but we are not. And the shilling is merciless.
Secondly, I have a great deal of technical expertise in certain areas. If the sub is not anonymous, I will not comment because I may be recognized. I recognized one of my former colleagues several times even though he was posting in QRV. If he had had a username, I would have been able to identify him.
So, if you want input from the likes of me, you need more anonymity.
