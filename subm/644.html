Sounds great.

In case my previous comment wasn't very clear, the conversion bolt is a drop in mod, replacing the existing AR bolt that came with your gun, that allows you to shoot 22lr instead of 5.56. 22lr is 10-30% the price of 5.56 right now, and much easier to find. And, most importantly, this is in no way a permanent mod. You can switch back and forth between bolts whenever, but it's usually a good idea to clean the barrel after shooting 22lr when you're switching back to 5.56. The conversion kit runs between $150 and $240, depending on where you buy it and how many mags come with it.

And on the subject of magazines... buy as many as you can now before Biden makes them NFA items requiring a $200 tax stamp... each. Which reminds me, time to order some more mags. Check out gunmagwarehouse.com for mags, Lancer mags are pretty much the best you can get. Steel mags are great too. Pmags and Hexmags are good, but the polymer feed lips can bend a little over time. If that's all you can find though, get them, they function flawlessly. DO NOT get Promag, they are garbage. I haven't tried MFT, but I've heard good things.

If you're looking for a red dot sight, try Holosun. If you're looking for a scope, try the Primary Arms 1-6x. If your new toy didn't come with iron sights, you really can't go wrong with Magpul MBUS. These are all high value budget options.

Don't put a verticle fore grip on it. That's not legal on an AR pistol... big felony. There are lots of angled grips out there if you think you need a fore grip.

You will benefit from adding a light. There are a lot of excellent choices in the light department. Hit me up if you want a specific recommendation.

Lastly, you need a sling. Magpul, Blue Force Gear, and Ferro Concepts all make top tier slings.
