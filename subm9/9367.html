I composed this post at the beginning of June:
For the last couple of months I have been planning my suicide. Part of that has been wrapping up my affairs, making a will, getting personal effects to specific people etc. SVF is precious to me and I wanted some way to help it survive. Hence the downloads and my vague advice about how long it will keep going. As some of you know, it runs on a server in my home. If/when I Catch The Bus it will probably stay up for a few days until someone just suddenly unplugs it.

Incidentally, I cannot imagine any reason why I would *ever* voluntarily take down SVF. If I'm alive, it's up. If it's down (for more than a glitchy couple of days), I'm dead. It's not da joos or Soros or the FBI or money woes.

I didn't intend to reveal this here because I don't want someone tracking me down IRL and "saving my life" (which imo is a shit thing to do, by the way, to someone who's serious about suicide, just to help yourself feel like A Good Person). But as a friend pointed out, reaching this point is basically bedrock. Niceties and worries and wants and what-ifs are all irrelevant. If I'm going to do it I'm going to do it regardless. If someone steps in front of me I can just wait till they're gone and do it anyway. So I might as well share this with you to satisfy some concerns and curiosity. Please don't doxx me.

I was expecting to leave this week, around 7 or 8 June. But circumstances have changed and it's been pushed back at least another couple of weeks. Or maybe never.

Sorry for the uncertainty and for any distress caused by this announcement.

Happy to discuss, but not the reason or the method.
I posted it, had second thoughts, and deleted it 5 seconds later. During that 5 second interval one of our regular users saw it, and responded via PM. I hope they don't mind that I mention this very private message, for which I am very grateful. (Do us all a favour and don't engage in whodunnit.) What's important is that they reminded me how important SVF is to me. It's my proudest achievement. I haven't really done anything else in my life to be proud of. I'm plagued by mental illness, I recently destroyed my marriage and lost my kids (hence the acute suicidality - it's always been low-level chronic), I'm actually an asshole: enough of an asshole to plan to abandon my tiny preschool-age kids to the insanity of my ex-wife for the rest of their lives. But I did make searchvoat.co when Voat was still alive and created SVF when it died. I passionately believe in free speech and I think SVF is kind of cool, even if there are only a couple of dozen of us.

Today is the perfect day to kill myself. I've spent several weeks researching and experimenting with methods. Said goodbye to friends and family. Bought the equipment. Have the house to myself for a least 24 hours. Ready to go. Not depressed (have some good psychotropic drugs) just have decided to stop playing The Game and check out. Set up Deadman's Handle emails and SVF updates.

I'm standing on the edge, the preparations are complete, and the only question left is what happens after death (and I am curious, have lots of theories, I just hope it isn't a cold black silent Hell for eternity, that would be annoying).

So.

At this point I am completely free. No physical, spiritual or mental pain. Unemployed, but with enough money in the bank for a while.

I'm still standing on the edge. Happy to jump. But if I don't, what could I do instead? Complete freedom. Any ideas?

Anyway, that's my confession. If I don't leap into the void maybe I'll start to contribute to this place as a user. I was @FeathersMcGraw at Voat when I joined 7 years ago and that's who I am here, when I'm not doing admin-y things.

Greetings.
