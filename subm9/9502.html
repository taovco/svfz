Live has curious times that remind us there is much more happening than we can know or understand. As an Orthodox Christian I'm well aware of the need for a spiritual hospital, one that provides needed guidance in the middle of chaos, depression, despair.

Recent 'curious' times: Had a childhood friend. We stayed in touch with over the years, and for the most part no matter how long it was since we talked it seemed like no time had passed. Until Trump was elected. I suddenly discovered my friend had transformed from a fun loving person who was right of center, into an activist who was insane in that we couldn't have a normal conversation. There was a new 'Woke speak' that I had no clue at the time that everyone was supposed to bow to it's dictates. I was 'censored' for every word that that supposedly was a trigger. It was very bizarre and back then 2016, I had no idea how radical American politics had become, or how much Marxism had made inroads. My friend strongly advised me to learn more and I can't even repeat the crap that was said. Anyway, over next few years since that conversation I called 2 or 3 times. Each time the short calls were very awkward, like talking to a stranger. So I stopped calling altogether. More years passed and I was quite sad that I had lost a childhood friend because of politics. I was very heartbroken tbh, I prayed in that I was letting go of the friendship but was sharing my grief with God. We hadn't spoken in probably 4 years and I didn't see that we ever would again.

Not so long after that (like less than a week) I was given a message that 'your friend X' called. Because they had the same name as someone that was friends with a family member who had passed, I figured it was them, calling to give me condolences. I was told that no it sounded more likely to be my friend. I was shocked given that I had just been grieving over the loss of that friendship. I called X and they told me that they had called to apologize for the way they treated me after Trump was elected. I shared how they have been on my mind so much and how I had given up on the friendship less than a week prior to them calling me.

They told me "I heard you". (and then described it)

So the connection remained in spite of how our personalities clashed, or how crazy the world had become. I've made a decision to avoid talking politics with them altogether, so that when we do converse, it can rise above all the pure shit that is going on, that has gone on and will continue to go on.
