I will not go into the details. I have lost family members to suicide, in different generations. I have lost friends to suicide.
The common denominator in the suicides close to me, is the following: After worrying and even being on suicide watch, you get help (desperation). You get people involved who is close to the person who I suicidal. Some people get angry; How could you even consider such horrible thing. You are selfish, think of the people close to you. You will ruin the lives of the people who loves you.

Time passes and things are getting better. The person (suicidal) appears to be doing good. They appear to be the same person before the (suicide chaos). They appears to be content and sometimes even happy. Sad to say, when the suicidal person has done a 180 and assures everyone that; I'm doing fine, I didn't know what I was thinking (suicide), you don't hafto worry about me, I'm content almost happy and doing much better. That's when they kill themselves.

The calm, content, almost happy (suicidal) person is feeling this way because they have already made up their mind. They are going to kill them selfs. No one can save a person from themselves.
