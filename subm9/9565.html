Original by Monty Python

Many people think that the use of the word "fuck" is in very bad taste. We have therefore engaged the services of Frick, Peters and Johnson, to provide us with an history of the word and examples of its usage.

Perhaps one of the most interesting and colorful words in the English language today is the word "fuck". It is the one magical word, just by its very sound, can describe pain, pleasure, love, and hate. Fuck, as most words in the English language, takes its name from the German word "fricken" which means "to strike".

In language, "fuck" falls into many grammatical catagories. It can be used as a verb, both transitive (John fucked Mary) and intransitive (Mary was fucked by John). It can be an active verb (John really gives a fuck) or passive verb (Mary really doesn't give a fuck) ; or an adverb (Mary is fucking interested in John), and as a noun (Mary is a terrific fuck). It can be used as an adjective (Mary is fucking beautiful): as you can see, there are very few words with versitility of "fuck".


Besides its sexual meanings, there are the following uses:
Fraud-----------------------------I got fucked by the car dealer.
Aggression-----------------------Fuck You!
Displeasure----------------------What the fuck is going on here?
Difficulty-------------------------I don't understand this fucking business.
Dismay----------------------------Oh, fuck it!
Incompetence-------------------He fucks up everthing.
Trouble---------------------------Well, I guess I'm fucked now.
Contempt-------------------------Biden's a worthless fucker.
Maternal--------------------------Motherfucker
Greetings-------------------------How the fuck are you?
Disgust----------------------------Fuck Me!
Confusion-------------------------What the fuck-------?
Dispair-----------------------------Fucked again.
Lost--------------------------------Where the fuck are we?
Disbelief---------------------------Unfuckingbelievable!
Retaliation------------------------Up your fucking ass!
Telling time-----------------------It's five fucking thirty!
Anatomical description---------He's a fucking asshole!
Politcal----------------------------Fuck Brandon!
It can be used in bussiness-----How the fuck did I wind up with this job?
And never forget General Custer's last words: "Where did all them fucking indians come from?"
Also, the famous last words of the mayor of Hiroshima: "What the fuck was that?"
And, last, but not least, the imortal words of the captain of the Titanic, who said, "Where is all this fucking water coming from?"
The mind fairly boggles at the many creative uses of the word. How can anyone be offended when you say "fuck"? Use it frequently in your speech and it will add to your fame and prestige.

Say it loud, say it proud!
