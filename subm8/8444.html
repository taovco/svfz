I have managed to parse some of SVF's data. Here is a short summary of SVF's stats, grouped by month. The data for this month is incomplete. For info on SVF's shutdown announcement, see this link (https://web.archive.org/web/20220522041 ... =24&t=8395 ).

List of attributes to the stats

SUBMISSION-COUNT: number of submissions
COMMENT-COUNT: number of comments
VERSE-COUNT: number of active subverses
VERSE-ITEM: number of items found in subverses, sorted by [percentage]/[subverse]
USER-COUNT: number of active users
USER-ITEM: number of items contributed by users, sorted by [percentage]/[username]
SUBMITTER-COUNT: number of active submitters
SUBMITTER-ITEM: number of submissions contributed by users, sorted by [percentage]/[username]
SUBMISSION-SCORE: score from submissions
COMMENT-SCORE: score from comments

As you can see from the SUBMITTER-ITEM section, SVF is a very small community. Most submissions are contributed by 7 users or so. We only have about 17 monthly submitters (SUBMITTER-COUNT), with 32 active subverses (VERSE-COUNT). When Voat.co was shut down, there were 200 active users (USER-COUNT) on this forum. Most of Voat's userbase did not make it here. For those who did, many left shortly thereafter, probably due to the format of forum.

I hope you get something out of this report. Both Voat and SearchVoat will always hold a very special place in my heart and in spirit. For those who have stuck around and are still here, I am sure they are more special to you all.
