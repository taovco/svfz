Uniting the Clans (GoatMatrix - SeachVoat - Upgoat)

Unread post by iSnark » Thu Feb 06, 2025 1:58 am

When the original VOAT Prime dissolved, goats were scattered to the winds. Since the breakup and dissolution of VOAT Prime, most of us have been attached to one of three spin-off projects, a few, like myself, have logons to each of the VOAT Prime spinoffs.

I've undertaken an effort to "Unite the Clans"... I'm making an internal public declaration of what this project is, and what this project isn't.

The main focus of this project is to reunite, as best as possible, each of the three spin-off groups. Out of the gate, there will NOT be an effort to consolidate sites into one specific VOAT website. The focus will be on getting as many goats as possible to establish logons at each of the spinoff sites. So you ask, "What does this buy us?"

The goal is to keep us together as a group across the three spinoff sites. The specific benefit will be that if one of the spinoff sites goes tits-up, goats can default to one of the two remaining active sites. Further consolidation means We'll be back to having one site again.

In my estimation of the three spinoff sites, one leads the pack in terms of user base size. The other two are maintaining and slowly growing their current user base. I don't have hard numbers as to the active participating members of each site, so I gave you my interpretation of each sites standing. (I could be wrong...)

I ask that goats support this initiative by establishing and maintaining logons at each of the three sites, and ideally, spend time at each of the sites, so that we even up the user bases among the three sites.

I'VE PROVIDED LINKS TO EACH VOAT SPINOFF SITE BELOW:

GoatMatrix: https://goatmatrix.net/
SearchVoat: https://searchvoat.co/forum/
Upgoat: https://www.voat.xyz/

If you're having issues creating/reactivating your user id at any of the sites, please make an attempt to reachout to the site mod, OR, in a worst-case scenario, ping me, and I'll assist in communicating between you and the sit mod(s).

I'm interested in your constructive thoughts relating to this effort.

"FUCK OFF, WE'RE FULL",
